About Our School

Specializing in TRADITIONAL Chinese Martial Arts, Sifu Mark Seidel focuses on teaching the external styles of Southern Hung-Gar & Northern Long Fist Boxing along with the internal style of Yang Style Tai Chi Chuan.

We utilize TRADITIONAL Chinese training methods that have been passed down through many generations for hundreds of years. These methods are not only physically and mentally demanding but also rewarding. Our objective is to produce a Martial Artist that has genuine skill with an emphasis on functionality and not simply appearance. We are skilled, experienced and experts in our field. We are only interested in training dedicated individuals with or without any experience.
